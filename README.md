# daily-calorie-tracker
This project is based on daily calorie tracking solution designed to help you monitor your nutritional intake and achieve your health goals. Whether you are looking to manage your weight, track your macros, or simply maintain a balanced diet, this application offers an intuitive interface for logging meals and visualizing your progress over time. Key features include a robust daily logging system, calculation of total calories consumed, and tools to set and monitor personalized calorie targets. This README.md will guide you through the setup, usage, and customization options available to make your calorie tracking experience seamless and efficient.
